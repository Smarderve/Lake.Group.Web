Lake Premix and Cement

Gulf Concrete and Cement Products (GCCP), Dar es Salaam's market-leading ready-mix concrete supplier since 2010.

01Company Introduction

Building East Africa's Future

Gulf Concrete and Cement Products Company Ltd (GCCP), established in 2010, is one of the leading suppliers of ready-mix concrete in Dar es Salaam, involved in many of the city's most prestigious construction projects.

Our ready-mix operations are powered by Sany International batching plants from China. In Dubai, Lake Group also operates MERM (Middle East Ready Mix LLC), one of the largest premix plants in the region.

02About the Company

Mission, Vision & History

Mission

To supply high-quality, reliable ready-mix concrete to Dar es Salaam's construction industry using modern, fully-automated batching technology.

Vision

To remain the region's market-leading ready-mix concrete supplier, supporting East Africa's most prestigious construction projects.

History

Gulf Concrete and Cement Products Company Ltd (GCCP) was established in 2010 in Dar es Salaam and has since grown into one of the city's leading ready-mix concrete suppliers, contributing to many of its most prestigious construction projects. Lake Group later extended its premix expertise to Dubai through MERM (Middle East Ready Mix LLC).
