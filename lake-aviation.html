Lake Group in the Air

Lake Aviation is Lake Group's aviation services division, sitting alongside Lake Oil, Lake Gas and Lake Lubes within the Group's Lake Energies family of companies.

As part of Lake Group's wider energy and industrial network across East and Central Africa, Lake Aviation extends the Group's presence into aviation-related services. Detailed company information is being finalised and will be published here as it becomes available.
