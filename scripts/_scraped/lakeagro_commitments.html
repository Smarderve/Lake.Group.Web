    THE PLEDGE

    SUSTAINABILITY

    Lake group considers contribution to the community as high priority, and is doing it for yours

    We create local labor, we train our employees and improve their professional know-how

    Lake Group considers the E/S aspects as top priority, as good compliance with E/S aspects ensures sustainable long-term successful operation.

    We make sure that our projects and activities are done according to the E/S highest standards.
