Aluminum Trailers Ltd

About The Company

ATL Limited (Aluminum Trailers Ltd), established in 2019, is the only manufacturer of high-quality aluminum trailers in Tanzania. We specialize in providing innovative and durable fuel transportation solutions.

Our Mission

To design and manufacture the highest quality trailers by fostering a culture of integrity, excellence, and continuous improvement.

Our Vision

To become the leading Trailer manufacturer in East and Central Africa providing sustainable solutions for your transport need. Focusing on Safety, Innovation and Customization.

OUR CORE VALUES

INTEGRITY

Honesty, transparency and accountability guide our actions and decisions. We build trust with our stakeholders by consistently delivering on our promises and maintaining strong moral compass.

PEOPLE

We believe our employees are our greatest asset. We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive work environment where everyone is empowered to grow, innovate and contribute to our success. We prioritize safety, professional development and a positive workplace culture.

SUSTAINABILITY

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices throughout our operations. From the materials we use to the manufacturing processes we employ; we strive to reduce our carbon footprint and support a greener future. Our commitment to sustainability extends to our products, ensuring they contribute to safer and more environmentally friendly transport requirements.

CUSTOMERS

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to understanding their needs and exceeding their expectations with superior products and services. Building strong, long-lasting relationships based on trust and mutual respect is essential to our success. We aim to be the preferred partner by consistently delivering value, quality and reliability.
