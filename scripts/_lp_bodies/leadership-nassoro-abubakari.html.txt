        <p class="lp-role">Project Manager · Lake Agro</p>
        <p class="lp-lede">Manages Lake Agro project delivery — greenfield development, agribusiness programmes and related Group project coordination.</p>
        <p>Nassoro Abubakari leads project delivery for Lake Agro, the Group’s agribusiness and greenfield development arm.</p>
        <p>The mandate covers planning and execution of agro programmes, coordination with Group support functions, and keeping delivery timelines aligned with commercial and operational goals.</p>
        <p>Lake Agro sits within Lake Group’s wider diversification strategy — extending the organisation’s capabilities beyond energy and logistics into agriculture-linked development.</p>
