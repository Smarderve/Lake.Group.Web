        <h1 class="lp-name">Sridhar Mani</h1>
        <p class="lp-role">Director of Digital Transformation</p>
        <p class="lp-lede">Owns enterprise technology architecture and digital strategy — from centralised SAP environments to logistics intelligence and cross-border data security.</p>
        <p>Across a Group that runs fuel depots, 1,200+ trucks, ICD/CFS yards and multi-country retail, digital systems are the nervous system. Mani’s mandate focuses on optimising Lake Group’s centralised SAP stack — the operational backbone for shipment orders, inventory and financial posting across units such as Lake Trans and AFICD.</p>
        <p>He embeds custom logistics and fleet-tracking intelligence so corridor movements stay visible from Dar to Ndola and beyond, and scales cross-border data security infrastructure protecting networks that connect Tanzania hubs with regional country operations.</p>
        <p>The goal is a single, resilient digital layer that lets manufacturing, energy and logistics executives act on the same real-time picture.</p>
