01Our Story

From a Single Fuel Company to a Regional Powerhouse

Lake Group was established in 2006 with the opening of its flagship company, Lake Oil: a humble beginning in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

Since inception, the growth story of Lake Group has been vertical, becoming one of the fastest-growing energy trading, logistics and construction supply material conglomerates in East and Central Africa and the Middle East.

Today, Lake Group distributes petroleum products in 8 countries, owns storage facilities across the region, manufactures lubricants and ready-mix concrete, and operates a fleet of more than 1,200 trucks.

With a diverse mix of 21 nationalities and a total workforce of 30,000+ employees, Lake Group continues to grow with purpose.
