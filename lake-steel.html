Lake Steel Ltd.

Lake Steel

Tanzania's first producer of HS-CR reinforcement steel bars, with a fully computerised rolling mill and 100,000 MT annual capacity.

01Company Introduction

First in Tanzania. Built to Last.

Lake Steel Ltd. is the first company in Tanzania to introduce unique High Strength, Corrosion Resistant (HS-CR) reinforcement steel bars, a game-changer for the East African construction industry.

Our state-of-the-art, fully computerised and automatic Steel Rolling Mill plant has a production capacity of up to 25 tonnes per hour, translating to an annual capacity of 100,000 metric tonnes of high-quality steel bars.

  • ✓Tanzania's first HS-CR reinforcement steel bar producer
  • ✓Fully computerised, automatic rolling mill
  • ✓25 tonnes/hour mill capacity, 100,000 MT/year output
  • ✓HS-CR bars maintain strength up to 600°C (vs. 350°C standard)

02About the Company

Mission, Vision & History

Mission

To manufacture high-strength, corrosion-resistant steel products that meet national and international standards, in a safe and efficient manner.

Vision

To be East Africa's leading manufacturer of advanced reinforcement steel, driving innovation in construction materials across the region.

History

Lake Steel was established as part of Lake Group's expansion into industrial manufacturing, becoming the first company in Tanzania to introduce HS-CR reinforcement steel bars. Its fully computerised rolling mill was purpose-built to serve the growing demands of East Africa's construction sector.
