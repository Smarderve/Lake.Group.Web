Gulf Aggregates

Gulf Aggregates

Operating our own quarry at Lugoba, Tanzania, supplying aggregate to Lake Group's own concrete operations and the wider construction industry.

01Company Introduction

Quarrying the Region's Building Blocks

Gulf Aggregates operates Lake Group's own quarry at Lugoba, Tanzania, with two aggregate crushing plants of 250 tons/hour capacity each, producing approximately 30,000 m³ of aggregate monthly.

Aggregate produced at Lugoba supplies Lake Group's own consumption — including Gulf Concrete and Cement Products' (GCCP) ready-mix operations — as well as the wider construction industry.

02About the Company

Mission, Vision & History

Mission

To responsibly quarry and supply high-quality construction aggregate to Lake Group's operations and the regional construction industry.

Vision

To be a dependable, high-capacity aggregate supply source underpinning Lake Group's construction materials businesses.

History

Gulf Aggregates was developed alongside Gulf Concrete and Cement Products (GCCP) to secure a reliable, own-quarried aggregate supply for Lake Group's ready-mix concrete operations in Dar es Salaam, with quarrying carried out at Lugoba, Tanzania.
