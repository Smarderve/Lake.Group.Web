Lake Lubes Ltd.

Lubricants & Lube Products

High-quality automotive, industrial and specialty lubricants manufactured and distributed across East and Central Africa.

01Lake Lubes Ltd.

Engineered for Performance

Lake Lubes Ltd., established in 2014, manufactures and distributes a comprehensive range of lubricants and greases serving automotive, industrial and specialty applications across East and Central Africa.

Our lubricants are formulated to meet international standards, providing superior protection and performance even in Africa's most demanding operating environments.
