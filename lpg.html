Powering homes, restaurants and industry across 7 East African countries with safe, reliable LPG cylinders.

01Lake Gas Ltd.

Clean Energy for Every Kitchen

Lake Gas Ltd. is East Africa's trusted LPG bottling and distribution company, operating across 6 countries. From domestic cooking to large-scale commercial kitchens and industrial applications, Lake Gas provides safe and reliable liquefied petroleum gas.

Our innovative composite cylinder range, launched in Dar es Salaam, marks a new revolution in cooking gas technology, combining safety with aesthetics.
