About Lake Group

A Conglomerate Built on Grit and Vision

Founded in 2006 from a single fuel outlet in Dar es Salaam, Lake Group has grown into one of East and Central Africa's largest energy, logistics and industrial conglomerates.

Today, with 30,000+ employees across 21 nationalities, 1,200+ trucks, 152 fuel stations and 20+ subsidiaries, Lake Group powers everyday life across Tanzania, Kenya, Zambia, Rwanda, Burundi, DR Congo, Ethiopia and Mozambique.
