Regional Presence

Spread Across Every Corner of East & Central Africa

Lake Group is geographically spread across every region of Tanzania and neighbouring countries of Zambia, DRC, Burundi, Rwanda and Kenya, with operations in Ethiopia, Mozambique and a major premix plant in Dubai.

Our petrol stations are instantly recognisable in eye-catching blue, white, yellow and red, the same bold Lake Group colours you'll see on the map below.
