Major Projects

A showcase of landmark construction, infrastructure and supply projects delivered by Lake Group across East Africa.

Portfolio

Delivering East Africa's Development

From multi-million dollar construction projects to long-haul logistics contracts, Lake Group has the scale, expertise and equipment to deliver.

InfrastructureCompleted

Tanga LPG Import Terminal East Africa's Largest

Lake Gas built East Africa's largest LPG storage terminal at Tanga commissioned at 1,000 MT and later expanded to 3,000 MT with a dedicated single point mooring buoy that lets ships discharge directly to the terminal.

TanzaniaLPG Gas
LogisticsOngoing

Pan-African Petroleum Supply Chain

Lake Oil and Lake Trans jointly operate one of East Africa's largest petroleum supply chains, distributing fuel to 8 countries with 1,200+ trucks running 24/7.

8 CountriesFuel & Logistics
ManufacturingOngoing

Lake Steel Rolling Mill, Tanzania First

Tanzania's first HS-CR rebar producer. The fully computerised rolling mill at Visiga, Kibaha runs at up to 25 tonnes per hour 100,000 MT annual capacity and its bars are approved for use in government projects.

TanzaniaSteel Manufacturing
Real EstateCompleted

MERM Ready Mix, Dubai Expansion

Middle East Ready Mix LLC (MERM), Lake Group's Dubai-based premix concrete operation, has been producing ready-mix concrete since 2005 and serves major construction projects across Dubai.

Dubai, UAEConcrete
Port LogisticsOngoing

African Inland Container Depot Network

AFICD operates strategic inland container depots in Tanzania, Zambia and Mozambique, extending port capacity into the hinterland and enabling efficient cross-border trade.

TZ / ZM / MZContainer Services

LPG Composite Cylinder Launch

Lake Gas pioneered the introduction of composite LPG cylinders in East Africa, non-explosive, lightweight and translucent, revolutionising domestic cooking gas safety.
