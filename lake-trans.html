Lake Trans

1,200+ truck fleet providing bulk liquid transport and regional cargo haulage across East and Central Africa.

01Company Introduction

The Backbone of East Africa's Supply Chain

Lake Trans Ltd. is one of East Africa's largest logistics operators, running a fleet of 1,200+ trucks for bulk liquid haulage, general cargo and regional cross-border transport.

From petroleum tankers to dry cargo, our transport division ensures timely, safe and cost-effective delivery to destinations across Tanzania, Kenya and Zambia, and throughout the wider region.

  • ✓Bulk petroleum and liquid haulage
  • ✓Dry cargo and containerised freight
  • ✓Cross-border regional transport
  • ✓Real-time fleet tracking capability
  • ✓Experienced drivers and route managers

02About the Company

Mission, Vision & History

Mission

To deliver safe, reliable and efficient haulage services across East and Central Africa, in a manner that safeguards our people, cargo and the environment.

Vision

To be the region's most trusted logistics partner for bulk liquid and general cargo transport.

History

Lake Trans grew alongside Lake Oil's petroleum distribution network, expanding from a small delivery fleet into one of East Africa's largest logistics operations with more than 1,200 trucks and roughly 18 years of operating experience across Tanzania, Kenya and Zambia.
