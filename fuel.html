Lake Oil Ltd.

Fuel & Petroleum Distribution

One of Tanzania's top 5 petroleum distributors, supplying quality fuel across 8 countries in East and Central Africa.

01Lake Oil Ltd.

Keeping Africa Moving

Lake Oil Ltd. is the flagship company of Lake Group and one of Tanzania's top 5 distributors of petroleum products. Founded in 2006, it has established a strong footprint across all of East and Central Africa.

We supply white petroleum products — petrol and diesel — through retail stations, bulk delivery and strategic storage facilities across the region, and provide bunkering services for vessels calling at the port of Dar es Salaam.

  • ✓Retail fuel stations, 85+ across Tanzania and the region
  • ✓Bulk petroleum supply for corporate and government clients
  • ✓Own oil storage facilities in Tanzania, Kenya, Burundi and DRC
  • ✓Competitive pricing backed by regional supply chain scale
  • ✓Quality products meeting national and international standards
